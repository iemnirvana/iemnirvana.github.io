NIRVANA 2019


Institute of Engineering and Management, Kolkata has always been one of the most reputed colleges in West Bengal and we would love to keep up with the glory that has been maintained for the last 30 years.
This year for the first time B.C.A. and M.Sc.(IT) department of the Institute of Engineering and Management, Kolkata is organising a fest from 8th April 2019 to 12th April 2019 at Ashram Campus.


Nirvana 2019 is a platform where students from various colleges of Kolkata and beyond can exhibit their talent and creativity, and compete with others to show their potentials.
Here, the desire to earn success will lead the students to give their fullest in various sectors like Robotics, Coding, Painting, Photography and many more. It is our endeavour to challenge participants to think, ideate, innovate and push themselves to go for that extra mile.
